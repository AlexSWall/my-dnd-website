This wiki uses a templating language to create wikipages, broadly modelled on the Wikipedia/WikiMedia templating style.

The following templating options are currently available:

  • sections/headings,
  • images,
  • links (with custom text),
  • lists (unordered),
  • paragraphs, and
  • basic text styling (bold & italics).
Furthermore, a table of contents is automatically generated immediately before the first heading (excluding the automatic heading at the beginning of the document).

Templating Guide

The following details how to utilise the templating options available when writing a wikipage.

Headers

To insert a header, write text of the form

== Header 1 ==

...Insert other templating between headings...

=== Subheading 1 ===

. . .

=== Subheading 2 ===

. . .

== Header 2 ==

. . .
